the search for a single banality is over. the need is satisfied by 10,000%.

eleven phone calls later, 110 numeric values on the screen and 100 sheets in one package, there remains an unused surplus of 99% of the total.

the video work 11 110 100 99 deals with questioning and criticizing consumerism that capitalism has normalized since its beginning. 3-in-1 packaging, more for less, and other apparent savings render the natural order of consumers meaningless.

the consumer becomes a collector, a storekeeper in a sea of material things that remain unused, also losing their intended purpose. starting from personal experience, i track my purchases and fascinations with the methods promoting this problem into three parts. i use the visual language of dada film, primarily in the effects of stop motion animation and reversible shots, combining these techniques with dadaist poetry and the creation of ephemeral artifacts.
