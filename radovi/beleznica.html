the notebook is an ever-growing collection of recorded thoughts - a meeting of late blue hours and the urge to escape the spoken word.

and jealousy, which feeds the sadness

by the spoonful

just enough to taste the sweet flavor

false happiness like popping powder in a packet

suddenly chases away the bitterness

but truth and inevitability remain in the throat

in the end, beneath the indifference

we are betrayed by blue tongues

mind rushes in a navy-blue watch

swaying like a pendulum across dreambook pages

suddenly we’re both to blame, me

and your inappropriate request that i take flight

rewinding happens periodically it’s tied to family homes rooms for little girls and the attic terrace a view of neighboring yards, clouds, and the unspoken escaping actually happens in reverse back into old streets and habits into the warmth of stillness escaping into stillness

and memory is periodic a flash like a drop of bitterness in seasonal fruit mostly sweet, bruised from ripeness ripeness is actually soft and sticky soft from the knowledge that defenses are useless patient in dreams of change skin that ages and stretches, speckled with stains ripeness speckled with stains

and melancholy is periodic you’d find it in a warm july afternoon in a breeze that brings it but leads nowhere it stays in the eye behind tinted glasses to stay is actually a new departure a familiar path in a new time and time is always new new and periodic

sometimes it’s not enough to do something for yourself because doing it for yourself is still doing it for others

then it is comforting to know that the attempt failed

surges of solitude are necessary, hope is not arranged by chance encounters

objectively open to novelty from the shadows behind damp pages life is sometimes cinematic just as films can be lifelike. or maybe they aren’t separate at all

i function quite well in someone else’s crowd from the sidelines of my empty corner really quite well crowds in my eyes are peace in my head

i remember what never happened because it didn’t exist, i tried not to forget i feel the consequences of my non-actions not as a negation that absolves guilt your honor, she’s innocent and did nothing about it

invisible consequences hurt and pain can’t be seen ugly feelings are warm still nearby, they breathe and blur my vision and i no longer write poetry i guess i have no one left to address

poetry seeks beauty manners and sugary etiquette how improper it would be to surrender to write letters full of hatred emotional discharge doesn’t suit paper those are specialties of others porcelain, for instance, takes the lead

the one that doesn’t break yet so easily knows and wants to under a slightly clumsier hand it sulks, leaps into the abyss pulls along as it cuts white edges embrace red fingers selfishly but effectively

porcelain is no longer angry and no longer exists, but that’s easy to forget spoken words don’t cut or ache they stick and slip under the skin remembered like the first injustice and irretrievable cuticles

i was a good playmate

like a plastic doll with spinning eyes

who blinks away her unfairly scribbled dress

when needed, even a boyish army comrade

hearts in lead shells

resist the fires of warm tears

that choke from the throat and assault locked lungs

i was the answer many times

but i never liked asking questions
